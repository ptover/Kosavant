What the audit reviews

The brand audit looks at the customer-facing pieces that decide whether people understand, trust, and buy from the brand.

  • Homepage and product presentation
  • Offer clarity and pricing perception
  • Brand story and trust signals
  • Drop/campaign messaging
  • Social-to-site conversion path

What you get from the audit

The goal is not just criticism. The audit should leave you with a clearer order of operations and action items that can be executed.

  • Main friction points
  • Quick wins for credibility
  • Messaging improvements
  • Launch and content priorities
  • Recommended next steps

Best fit

The brand audit is best when you already have a product, website, social page, or launch idea that needs a sharper review.

  • Existing clothing brands
  • New brands before launch
  • Founders preparing a drop
  • Brands with traffic but low conversion
