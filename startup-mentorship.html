Mentorship focus

Mentorship sessions help you sort through the noise and decide what matters next. The work is practical: clarify the offer, choose priorities, and avoid spending energy in the wrong place.

  • Idea validation
  • Offer structure
  • Launch planning
  • Brand and customer clarity
  • Execution priorities

Common questions

Founders often come in with too many ideas, unclear positioning, or a launch that needs structure. A mentorship session helps put those pieces into a clean order.

  • What should I build first?
  • How should I present this?
  • What should I charge?
  • What should I fix before launch?
