Privacy Policy

This page explains how KO Savant handles information submitted through the website, including consultation requests, payment handoffs, and client feedback updates.

What information is collected

  • Name, email address, business details, and message content when you submit a consultation or feedback form.
  • Service context you choose inside the payment portal so clients know what they are paying for.
  • Payment details entered on Stripe are processed by Stripe, not stored directly on this static website.

How the information is used

  • To respond to consultation requests and client follow-ups.
  • To review client feedback and updates before any testimonial is featured publicly.
  • To coordinate secure payment collection through Stripe, Cash App, or Bitcoin when requested.

Payments

KO Savant uses Stripe-hosted checkout for card and wallet payments. Apple Pay, Google Pay, Cash App Pay, bank transfer, Link, and card options are shown by Stripe when supported on the client’s device and browser. KO Savant does not store full card details on this website.

Feedback and testimonials

Client reviews and updates submitted through the site are screened before being featured publicly. Submitting feedback does not guarantee publication. If you check the box giving permission to feature your feedback, KO Savant may contact you before posting it.

Third-party services

This website uses third-party services such as FormSubmit for message delivery and Stripe for hosted checkout. Their processing is governed by their own privacy and security policies.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, data handling, or a payment made through the site, contact contact@kosavant.com.
